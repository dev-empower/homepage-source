Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DevEmpower Company Limited ("DevEmpower", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our websites, applications, and Discord integrations (including bots and any interaction/verification endpoints).

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with our services, we may collect:

  • Discord account information you or Discord provide to us when you install or interact with our application, such as your Discord user ID, username, and the ID of the server (guild) where our application is used.
  • Interaction data sent by Discord to our interaction endpoint when you use commands or components of our application (e.g. command name and parameters you submit).
  • Contact information you voluntarily provide, such as your email address, when you reach out to us directly.
  • Technical data such as log data, IP address, and browser/device information collected automatically when you visit our website.

2. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and provide the features of our website and Discord integrations.
  • To verify requests and secure our application against abuse.
  • To respond to your questions and support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate our services (e.g. hosting providers), or when required by law. Data necessarily flows to and from Discord as part of how Discord's platform and API work.

4. Data retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at tungta@devempower.com.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at tungta@devempower.com.
